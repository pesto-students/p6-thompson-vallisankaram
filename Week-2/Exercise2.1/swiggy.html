What are the services of Swiggy?

Founded in July 2014, Swiggy is based in Bangalore and operates in 500 Indian cities as of September 2021. Besides food delivery, Swiggy also provides on-demand grocery deliveries under the name Instamart, and a same-day package delivery service called Swiggy Genie.

What is the purpose of Swiggy?

Swiggy provides an online platform for ordering from a wide range of listed neighbourhood partner restaurants and has its own fleet of delivery personnel who pick up the orders from the partner restaurants and deliver them to the customers at their doorsteps.
